About me

I'm a PhD Candidate at the University of Kent School of Economics under the supervision of Dr. Alfred Duncan and Professor Miguel Leon-Ledesma.

I've collaborated on the UNCTAD's World Investment Report of 2022 and 2023, as well as co-authored the paper on the evolution of digital MNEs with Dr. Amelia Santos-Paulino and Dr. Claudia Trentini.

My Research interests: Monetary policy, exchange rate, simulated models.
